What is Electro?

Electro characters in Genshin Impact can prove extremely useful, creating powerful Elemental Reactions that quickly eliminate waves of enemies. These electrifying characters often shine with Hydro and Cryo allies, which makes them highly versatile to many team comps.

ELECTRO ABILITIES

Causes an explosion that deals AoE
Pyro DMG with high knockback.

Deals AoE Cryo DMG and reduces the
Physical RES of enemies caught in the
AoE by 40%.

Deals AoE Electro DMG while spreading
the Electro status to nearby enemies.

Creates an Electro shard. Picking it
up creates an Electro shield around
the character.
